Privacy Policy

What It Covers

This privacy policy covers how MATRIKA treats information that we collect and receive, including information related to your past use of MATRIKA website information and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available. This policy does not apply to website links provided here. These websites have their own privacy and information collection policy which can be viewed by clicking on the respective links of these websites.

Information Collection & Use

MATRIKA website collects personal information when you register with the website, you use our services, or when you visit different pages. We combine information about you which we obtain from various sources. We automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookie information, and the page you request. We use this information to better the website experience for users, focus the areas which are requested more often than others, improve our services, provide anonymous reporting for internal clients, and/or conduct research.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We strictly do not rent, sell or share personal information about you with other people and websites.

Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information

You may at any time add, edit or delete your account and/or personal information from our servers and records. The information stored in our log servers about your access to pages, your IP address, and/or other information may remain in our servers even after your deleting your account from the website.

Confidentiality and Security

We limit access to personal information about you to our personnel who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy at any time. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your account or by placing a prominent notice on our website.
